About Corn Unblocked

Hello! You probably don't know me. I'll try to keep it that way, but you could probably find my name pretty easily if you tried. I created this site because I believe the internet should be free. If you don't share my opinion, please don't try to hinder my progress. The internet is far to powerful for that.
Whoever you are, thank you for visiting my site! Even if you don't approve of my motives, your acknowledgement of my presence is a reward in itself.
Find me on GitHub here if you have an problem with the site. Leave an issue. Trust me, I really don't have a pressing priority other than reading your feedback.
